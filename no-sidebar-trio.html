Trio Warrior

An Intense 3D All-Terrain Combat Game

About: Game Pitch

"Rock, Paper, Scissors!" is a cherished children's game. Children grow up. But has the game grown up any? Well, that's where Trio Warrior: A Mancer's Path comes in! Play RockPaperScissors in the large and changing world of Ropascis, by means of a dynamic,fast-paced and diverse combat system based, purely off of that children's game you knew! Tackle enemies of various strengths and sizes, as they attack you up close, from afar, or simply all around them, with unique offensive and defensive traits to boot; Your arsenal in this endeavor is a large one, from basic melee, ranged and AoE attacks to powerful abilities that unhinge your full powers as a Mancer to both Pack a Punch in and Prone the Pace of combat. Build and lose levels based on your proficiency of the elements and Survival 101: Staying Alive. Be Wary: The land gradually rejects the weak-levelled! Can you stay true to A Mancer's Path, and conquer Ropascis before she deems you mediocre in her meritocracy?

Special Features

Trio Warrior boasts a plethora of special features within the game, though all may not be readily explained to the player at first. To browse a fuller list of features, view the documentation or watch the technical video via the links Below. This section showcases the most prominent Special Features of the Game:

  • Procedural Terrain World: Have a New Large Arena every run!
  • Difficulty Scaling: Enemies scale with Player Levels
  • Enemy Variance: Meet enemies of any combination of 3 different traits with randomized level-scaled stats.
  • Rock,Paper,Scissor: The game's main theme is Rock, Paper, Scissor. Reflected in Combat, AI, Skills, etc.
  • Procedural World: Music and Field changes with the 'Time of Day'
  • Multiple Levelling Paths: Find statues to level Movement, and activate platforms to level Combat.
